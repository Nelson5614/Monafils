About Monafils Institute

Shaping the future of chess in Lesotho through excellence in training, competition, and strategic thinking.

Our Story

A Legacy of Chess Excellence

Founded in 2020, Monafils Institute emerged from a vision to transform chess education in Lesotho. What began as a small training center has grown into the country's premier chess academy, producing national champions and international competitors.

Our commitment to excellence has attracted FIDE-rated masters and professional instructors who share our passion for developing the next generation of chess champions.

Our Mission

To cultivate strategic thinking and chess excellence in Lesotho through world-class training and competitive opportunities.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Africa's leading chess academy, producing international masters and fostering a culture of strategic excellence.

Our Values

Excellence, dedication, strategic thinking, and continuous improvement form the cornerstone of our training philosophy.
